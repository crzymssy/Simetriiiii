About Me

Cha Eun-woo, born Lee Dong-min on March 30, 1997, is a South Korean singer, actor, and model. He is best known for being a member of the popular K-pop boy group ASTRO, which debuted in 2016 under Fantagio Entertainment. Cha Eun-woo's visuals, often described as "the visual representative of K-pop," have earned him the nickname "Face Genius" due to his striking and symmetrical features, which have led to his widespread popularity in both the music and entertainment industries.

Cha Eun-woo is known for his charming and humble personality, which has contributed to his widespread popularity. He is often described as having a gentle demeanor and being hardworking and respectful. His relatable and endearing personality has made him a favorite among fans and fellow celebrities alike. In summary, Cha Eun-woo is an immensely popular multi-talented celebrity who has won the hearts of fans worldwide through his stunning visuals, acting prowess, and warm personality.
